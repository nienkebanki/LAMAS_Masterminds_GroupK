Announcement and model restriction

After a guess $g$, the observed public feedback pair $(F_B,F_A)$ eliminates worlds inconsistent with it. This corresponds to announcing the formula: $$\big(fb(g,c_B)=F_B\big)\ \wedge\ \big(fb(g,c_A)=F_A\big).$$

The updated model is obtained by restricting the set of worlds to those where the formula is true. Because announcements are public and truthful, the update result is common knowledge.
